# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Showing empathy toward other community members
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Violence, threats, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Posting sexually explicit or otherwise inappropriate content
- Disrupting project participation or collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers at: community@forgata.dev
